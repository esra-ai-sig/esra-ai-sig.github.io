Checklist preview

This local preview summarizes the checklist structure. Use the Nature article and table for the authoritative version and answer options.

A

Scope of LLM use

Record where LLMs enter the project and how much human oversight is involved.

B

Model or system details

Capture the model identity, access route, configuration, customization, and memory behaviour.

C

Prompts

Provide the prompts and any wider instructions that shape model behaviour.

D

Data inputs and privacy

Document how personal or sensitive data are handled, including consent and processing safeguards.

E

Validation and interpretation

Describe human validation and any post-processing that changes or filters LLM outputs.

F

Reproducibility

Share code, notebooks, scripts, or structured call records where disclosure is feasible.

G

Competing interests

Disclose funding, compute access, provider relationships, and other relevant support.
